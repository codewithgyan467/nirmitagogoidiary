“Love Is Simple”

By- Nirmita
On- 1st Oct. 2024
@ 0352 hours, a.m.
Place- Nazira, Assam, India

Love is simple…..it can be felt in simple things. Like, a Hi !! meaning…I was remembering you. Or….”কত আছা ? (where are you) “ meaning…. I was concerned about you…..

A call or a missed one…..meaning…I was missing you and was wanting to listen to your voice……

Love is just love…..it does not show proudness and does not dishonour others. It is not self seek…..and keeps no records of our negatives. It does not delight anything but rejoices the truth. It always protects, always trusts, always hopes and always perseveres……

Love never fails…. We will never know how lucky we are to spend the rest of our life with the other half of our soul……until we’ll have to spend their's without…..

Love…..
